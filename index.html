Wonderful Voices of Africa

  • Vision
At Wonderful Voices of Africa, our journey is rooted in a deep commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of orphans and the elderly. Founded 2015, we embarked on a mission to provide love, care, and support to those in need. What started as a humble endeavor has blossomed into a community-driven force for change.
By providing them with the support, resources and opportunities, we aim to enhance the quality of life for vulnerable elderly individuals, create a society where they are cherished for their wisdom and the children are equipped with the tools, they need to build a brighter future.
A world where vulnerable elderly individuals and young children thrive in nurturing and supportive environment.

What We Do?

We believe that we can enhance more lives with you

Healthy Food

Our NGO is committed to providing nutritious and wholesome meals to the elderly and orphans. We believe that access to healthy food is essential for their well-being, supporting their physical and mental health.

Pure Water

Access to clean and safe drinking water is a basic necessity. We ensure that the elderly and orphans under our care have reliable access to pure water, promoting their overall health and hygiene.

Health Care

We prioritize the health and well-being of the elderly and orphans through comprehensive healthcare services. Our dedicated team ensures regular check-ups, medical assistance, and a caring environment for those in need.

Primary Education

Education is a powerful tool for empowerment. We provide primary education and study materials to orphans, offering them the opportunity to build a brighter future. Our focus is on nurturing their potential and fostering a love for learning.

Residence Facilities

We offer safe and comfortable residence facilities for the elderly and orphans who may not have a stable living environment. Our goal is to create a supportive and caring home where they can thrive and feel secure.

Social Care

Emotional well-being is crucial for individuals in vulnerable situations. Our social care programs focus on providing companionship, emotional support, and a sense of community to both the elderly and orphans, fostering a supportive network.
